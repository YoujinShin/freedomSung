FREEDOM SUNG

Ethnographic fieldwork about freedom songs and protest dances served as tactics of liberation struggles in Johannesburg, South Africa

At various moments in South Africa's history, protesters inverted political expectations by confronting brutal repression, not with matching armory, but with song and dance. Freedom songs and protest dances served as tactics of liberation struggles against the ravages of war, against colonialism, and most notably against apartheid.
